**Project Overview**



This project analyzes sales data from Ferns N Petals (FNP) to understand revenue trends, customer behavior, and product performance using Excel.



**Objectives**

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Study customer purchase patterns
* Analyze revenue trends over time
* Support strategic business decisions



**Dataset Description**



The dataset includes order-level sales information such as:

* Order ID
* Order Date
* Customer ID
* Product Category
* Product Name
* Quantity
* Sales Amount
* Delivery Location



**Tools \& Techniques Used**



* Microsoft Excel
* Data Cleaning \& Preparation
* Excel Functions: SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, IF
* Date functions
* Pivot Tables \& Charts
* Conditional Formatting
* Dashboard design



**Key Analysis Performed**



* Total sales and revenue trends
* Sales by product category
* Top-selling products
* Sales by city/location
* Customer order frequency
* Average order value



**Key Insights**



* Identified high-revenue products and categories
* Certain locations contribute more to total sales
* Repeat customers generate significant revenue
* Sales increase during specific occasions/festive periods



**Deliverables**



* Cleaned Excel dataset
* Pivot-based reports
* Interactive Excel dashboard
* Actionable business insights



**Conclusion**



This project demonstrates the use of Excel for real-world sales analysis, helping businesses understand customer demand, improve product strategy, and increase revenue.
