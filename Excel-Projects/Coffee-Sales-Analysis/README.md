**Project Overview**



This project analyzes sales performance of a coffee shop using Excel. The goal is to understand customer buying patterns, peak sales periods, product performance, and revenue trends to support better business decisions.



**Objectives**



* Analyze total sales and revenue trends
* Identify peak sales days and hours
* Find best-selling products and categories
* Understand customer purchase behavior
* Support data-driven decisions for inventory and staffing



**Dataset Description**



The dataset contains transactional coffee shop sales data, including:



* Order Date \& Time
* Store Location
* Product Category
* Product Name
* Quantity Sold
* Unit Price
* Total Sales



**Tools \& Techniques Used:**



* Microsoft Excel
* Data Cleaning (removing blanks, formatting dates)
* Excel Functions:
* SUM, AVERAGE, COUNT, IF
* Date \& Time functions
* Pivot Tables \& Pivot Charts
* Conditional Formatting
* Dashboard creation



**Key Analysis Performed**



* Sales by day of the week
* Sales by hour of the day
* Monthly revenue trend
* Sales comparison across store locations
* Best-selling products by quantity and revenue
* Average order value



**Key Insights**



* Identified peak sales hours and high-traffic days
* Certain product categories contribute most to revenue
* Some store locations outperform others consistently
* Seasonal trends affect monthly sales



**Deliverables**



* Cleaned Excel dataset
* Pivot tables and charts
* Interactive Excel dashboard
* Business insights and observations



**Conclusion**



This analysis helps the coffee shop optimize staffing, inventory planning, and promotional strategies by understanding customer behavior and sales trends.
