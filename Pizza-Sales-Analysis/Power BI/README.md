**Project Overview**



This project analyzes pizza sales data to uncover revenue trends, customer ordering behavior, and product performance using Power BI.

The goal is to provide actionable business insights through an interactive and visually clear dashboard.



**Business Objectives**



* Analyze overall sales and order performance
* Identify top-selling pizzas and categories
* Understand customer ordering patterns by time
* Track revenue growth over time
* Support data-driven decision making for sales strategy



**Dashboard Highlights**



* Total Revenue \& Total Orders KPIs
* Sales by Pizza Size (L, M, S, XL, XXL)
* Top-Selling Pizzas by Quantity
* Orders Distribution by Hour
* Revenue Contribution by Category
* Cumulative Revenue Trend Over Time



**Tools \& Technologies**



* Power BI
* Power Query – Data cleaning \& transformation
* DAX – Calculated measures \& KPIs
* Excel / CSV – Source data



**Key DAX Measures Used**



* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Cumulative Revenue
* Revenue by Category
* Quantity Sold



**Key Insights**



* Large pizzas generate the highest revenue
* Peak order hours are during lunch (12–1 PM) and dinner (6–7 PM)
* Classic category contributes the highest share of revenue
* Sales show steady cumulative growth over time



**Dataset Description**



The dataset contains:

* Order details (date, time)
* Pizza name, size, and category
* Quantity sold
* Price and revenue information

(Public dataset used for learning and analysis purposes)
